Monkey  a common name that may refer to groups or species of mammals, in part, the simians of infraorder Simiiformes. The term  applied descriptively to groups of primates, such as families of new world monkeys and old world monkeys. Many monkey species  tree-dwelling (arboreal), although there  species that live primarily on the ground, such as baboons. Most species  also active during the day (diurnal). Monkeys  generally considered to  intelligent, especially the old world monkeys of Catarrhini.
